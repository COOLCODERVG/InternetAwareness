Types of Internet Risks

Click on a topic below to learn more about it.

Cyberbullying

Cyber Bullying is a kind of bullying or harassment that is done online. This is done by actual people. The intention like always is to harm the person on the other side except this time through a screen.

Identity Theft

Identity Theft is the fraudulent acquisition and use of a person's private identifying information, usually for financial gain.

Cybersecurity

Cybersecurity is the prevention or security of sensitive data that is stored within electronic devices.
